Questions

You ask. We answer.

How long does it take to get Onyx live? +

Most teams are issuing offer letters via voice within 30 minutes. Full integration with your existing HRIS, payroll, and identity provider typically takes 2–5 business days.

Does Onyx replace our current HRIS? +

Onyx can either sit on top of your existing HRIS as the command layer, or fully replace it. Most Growth-tier customers retire 4–7 legacy tools within 90 days.

What happens to our data? +

Your data stays in your chosen region (US, EU, or APAC), encrypted at rest with AES-256. We never train shared models on customer data — period.

Which integrations are supported? +

16+ first-class integrations across Workday, Rippling, BambooHR, Greenhouse, Lever, Slack, Microsoft 365, Okta, Google Workspace, ADP, Gusto, NetSuite, Jira, Notion, Zoom, and Linear.

How accurate is voice transcription in noisy environments? +

Onyx hits 99.1% word accuracy in office environments and 96.8% in conference rooms. We use on-device VAD plus dual-pass server transcription to handle noise and code-switching.

Can we restrict what Onyx can do automatically? +

Yes. Every action class — offers, terminations, payroll edits, policy changes — supports configurable approval thresholds, role-based scopes, and dollar limits.

Is there a non-voice interface? +

Of course. Every voice command has a typed equivalent and a click-through fallback. Voice is the fastest path, not the only one.

What does the free trial include? +

Full access to Growth-tier features for 30 days, up to 50 seats, with all integrations enabled. No credit card required and no implementation fees.
